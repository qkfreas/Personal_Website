IT Policy

Policy Statement

IT Policy is important with any IT organization. Whether the organization implements or just uses IT related equipement. Taking care of that equipement and ensuring that it is use properly is essential for smooth operations in every organization. Those who use IT equipement are expected to respect that equipement and ensure there is no damage.
Users are responsible for both the software and the hardware. Proper security will be provided on organization machines. Users are prohibited from disabling, modifing, or removing security software. It is never the recommendation of IT that users outside of the organization use internal equipement unless that equipement is secure for the purpose of non-organization use.

It is the overall goal of IT to provide services and security in the best interest for the organization.
