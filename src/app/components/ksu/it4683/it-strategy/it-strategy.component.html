IT Strategy

IT Strategy Roles

The purpose of IT Strategy is to provide guidence to IT professionals. The IT Strategy should cover, in depth, topics that are IT related.

Guidelines

  1. Create a Mission Statement - Understanding the purpose of IT is essential for an organization. This way, it is easier to determine the goals and responsibilities of IT.
  2. Create a SWOT Analysis - A SWOT analysis can provide insight into the strengths, weaknesses, oppurtunities, and threats posed internally and externally to IT. SWOT can help define what areas need to be improved within IT.
  3. Define Goals - The goals of IT should be clearly defined. The goals should be an improvement that helps the organization or IT.
  4. Provide Road Maps
  5. Define Key Components
  6. Understand Technology Impact - Technology is the future! Determining how that technology impacts an organization and the individuals, either employees or clients, in that organization should be clearly assessed.
  7. Understand Pathway to Success - Understanding the best way to align what IT to help the organization be successful is important for any business. IT is a big investment and there should be a clear understanding on its overall goal. [3]
