IT Governance

Roles

In IT, there are many different types of roles to play. The one that will be used here is one found in the COBIT handbook.
Each part of the workflow plays an important part in IT Governance and no part should be overlooked.
It is important that the needs of the client are met, IT Stratigic Alignement will help work through any and all business needs and concerns.
After the business needs are determined, IT Value Delivery will bring forward a product or service specially designed for the client.
Risk Management will evaluate the processes after they are put into place and determine the risks involved, if any.
After risk has been assessed, the current process will go throuhg a Performance Measurement to determine if it is the right process for the client's needs.
